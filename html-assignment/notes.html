HTML Notes

body
The body is the main element, it is where most of the content will be.
h2
The h2 tag is a second level header, headers help describe the text that will follow.
q
The q tag quotes text, useful when needing to quote something a person has said.
em
The em tag makes the chosen text in italic.
ol
The ol tag creates an ordered list, the list is also numbered.
